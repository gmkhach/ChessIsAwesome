Welcome to ChessIsAwesome.com!

Learn how to play the most celebrated board game of all times. Read about chess, study theories, review historic games of the past, and check out the latest news in the chess world. Start your day with a quick brain exercise trying to solve the Daily Puzzle while your coffee is brewing (don't be mad at me if you forget about your coffee). Take a break and read a short story, or an anecdote, about the famous people in chess history to relax from the long hours of work or study.

If you are new in the game, you can find many useful introductory lessons in the LEARN section. Lessons for intermediate and advanced level players, such as opening theories, tactics, middle game analysis, are planned to be added to this section later.

You can also go through many great games that have been played by GrandMasters of different eras, starting from mid-nineteenth century all the way up to today's top GMs. You will find these games in the LIBRARY of the website.

The HISTORY & STATISTICS section is designed to give in-depth presentation of the game's history, as well as show the statistics of the famous players' ratings and results. Chess is known for its interesting, amusing stories, many of which will be added to this section of the website in the near future.
